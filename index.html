Hellbound
Crime Drama Fantasy K-Drama

2021 ● 1 Season - 6 Episodes

People hear predictions on when they will die. When that time comes, a death angel appears in front of them and kills them. Hellbound occurs in 2023–27 in South Korea. An otherworldly face called an angel suddenly starts to materialize to deliver prophesies called decrees that condemn certain individuals to Hell at a specific future time, either seconds or years away. Three hulking supernatural monsters appear at almost the exact time to maul and incinerate the person's body in a sort of spectacular show of force called a demonstration. Two organizations in cahoots, the cult-like New Truth Society and the gang-like Arrowhead group, gain power by playing on people's fears.

Cast Yoo Ah-in Kim Hyun-joo Park Jeong-min extend Director Yeon Sang-ho
