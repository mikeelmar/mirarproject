2021 ● TV-PG 16+

Comedy Romance K-Drama

Hyuk is hospitalized with Hepatitis B. While the other patients as well as his own parents keep their distance, nurse Da-eun ignores them and willingly takes care of him. As time goes by, Hyuk and Da-eun grow closer with the overworked Da-eun even eating and resting on his hospital bed.Faced with real-world opportunities and challenges, a couple endures the highs and lows of trying to make a long-distance relationship survive.
