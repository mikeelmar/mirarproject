01

After escaping death - twice in one night, Benjo Malaya (Arjo Atayde), a small-town barber turned recounts the events that led him down the dark and dangerous path of becoming the Governor's "Bagman".

02

To secure his family's future, Benjo allows his barbershop to be used as a drop off and pick up point for three unassuming Styrofoam boxes. Not knowing that the boxes contain "grease money" for the vast smuggling network in the province, Benjo is assaulted by Cito (Allan Paule), the Governor's right hand, for his involvement in the illegal operation. At knife point, Benjo's loyalty is tested. His decision impresses Cito and paves the way for Benjo's mentorship to be part of the Governor's "inner circle".

03

Cito trains Benjo in the ways of the Bagman: leverage, manipulation, and violence. Three years later, Benjo must use all that he learned from Cito to regain control of his life from the Governor who has already betrayed him. Benjo's first move is to strike a deal with Leon, the Bagman of the Governor's political rival.

04

With just months from his re-election campaign, Governor Arthur Alcantara is scrutinized for his alleged ill-gotten wealth and corruption practices by the influential broadcaster reporter, Sonny Liwanag. Benjo volunteers and succeeds in silencing Sonny before the truth about the Governor is fully exposed. By serving his master well, Benjo regains the Governor's trust. Now, he is in the perfect position to execute his plan to bring the Governor down.

05

One by one, Benjo's offensive against the Governor unfolds. The Governor begins to lose grip on his financial benefactors, political backers and even the affection of the women in his life. Benjo's plan pushes the Governor to the brink of backing down from the elections but memories of his climb to power with Cito, sways the Governor's decision to fight on. With his failure and at Leon's gunpoint, Benjo has to resort to a desperate move to bring down the Governor and save his family.

06

Benjo has only three days to find the corpse of Attorney Joselito Fernando, the first kill of the Governor and Cito during their rise to power. But unknown to Benjo, the noose around his neck is getting tighter as the Governor and Attorney Alex suspects a mole among their ranks.

07

Benjo's search for evidence against the Governor is stifled when he is called in by Attorney Alex to do a loyalty check on the 12 Mayors of Magdiwang. During which Benjo uncovers information pointing him to the location of Joselito Fernando's corpse. Benjo races to the site. In doing so, he has confirmed Attorney Alex's (Yayo Aguila) suspicion that he is the traitor among them. The Governor orders the capture of Benjo.

08

Finally, Benjo digs up Joselito Fernando's briefcase which contains enough evidence to bury the Governor for murder but he is captured by the Governor's men. A bloody mess, Benjo finds himself face to face with the Governor who is on the verge of killing him. Benjo resorts to the last card up his sleeve, to pin his treachery on Tres (Angelica Yap), the Governor's youngest girlfriend.

09

The naive Tres falls into Benjo's trap which implicates her as Mariano's (Cris Villanueva) mole, spurring the Governor's anger and violence towards her. Atty. Alex, on the contrary, is suspicious of Benjo's shticks and is determined to pound the truth out of him, but Benjo is unyielding. However, things go south when the Governor ordered Benjo to kill Tres. Benjo tries his best to save Tres but accidentally kills her after she finds out that Benjo set her up.

10

After Sam refuses to leave Magdiwang with Benjo, Leon shows up at Ka Romy's house pretending to be the professor's former student. Adamant to kill Benjo, Leon stabs Benjo's leg and points a gun at him. With his life in peril, Benjo then remembers how a simple favor from the Governor led to the crumble of his marriage. As Leon prepares to pull the trigger, he receives a call from Mariano ordering him to spare Benjo's life, for Mariano cooks a plan that only Benjo can execute seamlessly.

11

At the Governor's party, Benjo transports a bag not to be opened until Mariano says so. Leon keeps Benjo's family as hostage. As the night continues, Benjo gets distracted by the drunk Dos. As he tries to calm her down, Benjo recounts the events which led to his loss of the Governor's favor. Dos opens Benjo's bag. There's a bomb in the bag.

12

Benjo seeks the Governor's help to dispose of the bomb as well as to save his family. In return, Benjo saves the Governor's life on the shoot out during the filing of candidacy, or not. The Governor died and the "invisible hands" groom Benjo to be his successor. He will be proclaimed as the new Governor, but did he really win?

01

As Benjo hovers between life and death, he encounters his late uncle Dencio's apparition, giving him a perspective on why he should continue fighting and living. When Benjo regains his consciousness, he faces the reality in front of him: that he needs to go back to Magdiwang and be a puppet governor. His first order of business? Build the shady Magdiwang Port.

02

As news on the displacement of multiple residents linked to the construction of Magdiwang Port breaks out, protesters criticizing the said project sprang up like mushrooms. And, one of the most vocal and influential critics among the opposition is Father Sebastian. To shut the priest's mouth, Benjo resorts to blackmail. Meanwhile, Emman looks for a way to persuade his boss to give him his job back.

03

After his brutal murder of Father Sebastian, Emman impresses Congressman Matias and earns himself a position within the Congressman's inner circle. Meanwhile, Benjo has to deal with the backlash of the priest's death from the public and the media, which is spearheaded by a vlogger under the name of "Alyas Pepe."

04

Despite all of the information regarding Angela Go Ming, Congressman Matias, and the Triad syndicate, Benjo must delve deeper into their world to gain hard evidence to bring them down. Benjo gains Go Ming's trust by halting the inquiry into the construction of the Magdiwang Port. With Benjo's success, Angela's confidence in him rises that leads to his discovery of her vault which he believes contains all the evidence that he would need against the Triad.

05

As Emman keeps an eye on Benjo's every move, he discovers Atty Alex's hiding place. Emman accidentally kills Atty. Alex, leaving Benjo in distress. The key to Benjo's escape plan is now dead. Trying to save as much of his original plan, Benjo realizes that if he can still win with Emman behind his back. Benjo attempts to conspire with Emman. He tries to convince Emman to work with him. Benjo plants a seed of doubt in Emman's mind. That he is worthless in the eyes of the Congressman. That in the end, he can easily be disposed like garbage. Initially, Emman does not ...

06

After Benjo saved Emman's family from the bloody massacre, Emman comes around and accepts Benjo's deal. Benjo will teach Emman all of the things he needs to learn to become a skillful bagman. Now well-equipped, Emman returns to Congressman Matias, but now as Benjo's pair of eyes. While Emman tries to regain Congressman Matias's trust, Benjo is diving deeper into Go Ming's world. As Benjo succeeds in letting Go Ming's guard down, he takes this opportunity to look for any evidence that he may use to expose the Triad's criminal operations.

07

The divide between Angela Go Ming and Congressman Matias's alliance becomes even more glaring to Benjo. Together with Emman, Benjo exploits this weakness by exposing Matias's shell companies that deal with the smuggling of the country's natural resources. After, he convinces Angela Go Ming that Matias is a loose end that needs to be dealt with. At the same time, Emman convinces Congressman Matias that the exposè is part of Benjo and Go Ming's plan to eliminate him from the Triad. Triggered, Matias sets out to kill Benjo and Angela Go Ming. A Mexican standoff ensues ...

08

Benjo uses his birthday celebration as a diversion to escape from Angela Go Ming and her men. One by one, Benjo and his family manages to leave the Governor's mansion but Emman catches up to him and is out for revenge. At Emman's gunpoint, Benjo pleads for his life and his family's. But Emman shoots him in cold blood. After, Emman goes to Senator Paglinawan and exposes Angela Go Ming and the Triad's plans for the Magdiwang Port using evidence that he gained from Benjo's corpse. Angela Go Ming is arrested and the Magdiwang Port Project is shut down all thanks to Emman ...
