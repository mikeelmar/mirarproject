Locke and Key

2020-2021 ● TV-PG 18+

Drama Fantasy Horror

After Rendell Locke is murdered at the hands of former student Sam Lesser, his wife Nina decides to move with her three children, Tyler, Kinsey, and Bode, from Seattle to Matheson, Massachusetts, and take residence in Rendell's family home named Keyhouse. The children soon discover a number of mysterious keys throughout the house that can be used to unlock various doors in magical ways. They soon become aware, though, of a demonic entity that is also searching for the keys for its own malevolent purposes.

01
THE PREMIERE

Gabe forges an unholy alliance with Eden while Kinsey prepares for the premiere of the Savini Squad's horror film arrives. Duncan moves into Keyhouse with the rest of the Locke family while his fiancé Brian is staying in Tokyo for six months. Jackie starts forgetting the magic of the keys, as her eighteenth birthday is quickly approaching, which greatly concerns Tyler.

02
THE HEAD AND THE HEART

Tyler grows concerned over Jackie's memory loss, Gabe probes the Lockes information about the keys, and Nina meets a mansplainer at her new job. Nina starts her new job renovating the school theater at Matheson Academy, where she meets history teacher Josh Bennett. Bode becomes friends with a new girl at school named Jamie, and he tells her about the keys.

03
SMALL WORLD

Amid Duncan's unease over a new houseguest, Gabe drives a wedge between Kinsey and Scot while Bode finds an unusual key. Twenty-three years ago, a housekeeper at Keyhouse finds Erin using the Head Key and accidentally dislodges it, inadvertently trapping Erin inside of her own head. In the present, Erin moves into Keyhouse until her trust fund comes through.

04
FORGET ME NOT

With Duncan's emotional state deteriorating, Gabe confronts one of the Lockes, Tyler surprises Jackie on her birthday and Kinsey faces up to her fears. Duncan struggles to adjust to the return of his lost memories, resulting in extreme mood swings and hallucinations, causing the Lockes and Erin to realize that his best chance at a full recovery is to find the Memory Key.

05
PAST IS PROLOGUE

Gabe insinuates himself into Tyler's search for the Memory Key as Kinsey resolves to find out what is going on with Eden. Bode visits an old friend while Gabe visits Keyhouse in search of the music box in order to force Kinsey to make his key for him. In the process, Gabe learns of their efforts to restore Duncan's memories and becomes interested in Duncan's ability to make keys.

06
THE MAZE

At the annual Winterfest celebration, Kinsey tries to play it cool around Gabe while a key found by Bode and Duncan may offer a way to take down Dodge. Gabe asks Kinsey and Tyler how things went with Duncan and the Memory Key, but they tell him the key did not work and that he should leave. Later, Bode comes back from Nebraska and tells Tyler and Kinsey that there were two Dodges on the night of the shadow attack.

07
BEST LAID PLANS

Kinsey puts her acting skills to the test when the Lockes and their friends set a magical trap at the Keyhouse. Scot then arrives at Keyhouse, and the group discusses their plan to defeat Dodge. Gabe and Eden arrive with the false impression that they will be filming the sequel to The Splattering, and while Gabe is filming with Scot and Kinsey, Tyler uses the Anywhere Key on a nearby door to lead to the well house, where Gabe will be destroyed.

08
IRONS IN THE FIRE

As the origins of the Locke family magic come to light, Tyler and Kinsey look to protect their beloved ones from Gabe's expanding collections of pawns. In the present, the Lockes attempt to protect their remaining friends from Gabe as he uses his new Demon Key to turn people into demons. However, their efforts fail as Gabe turns Jackie into a demon. Tyler decides that they must craft a new key that can "unlock" the demon from a host's soul.

09
ALPHA AND OMEGA

While Tyler works on a way to help Jackie, Gabe gives Kinsey a deadline to meet his demands. Eden manipulates Josh and a shaken Nina reaches out. Gabe visits Kinsey and threatens to kill Nina unless Kinsey willingly turns into a demon. Meanwhile, Eden manipulates Josh into going with her to the Black Door, and she kills Javi after catching him spying on her, taking the Anywhere Key from him.

10
CLIFFHANGER

Escalating threats and devastating loss lead the Lockes into a ferocious showdown with Dodge. Back at the Keyhouse, Bode confronts a shocking visitor. While Tyler, Duncan, and Scot sneak into Gabe's house to steal the Demon Key, Kinsey refuses Gabe's offer and escapes with the Angel Key, which gives her wings. Tyler uses the Alpha Key to kill Mutuku and several other demons before Duncan discovers that, as the creator of the Demon Key, he can control the demons;

01
WELCOME TO MATHESON

A man, after receiving a phone call, takes a key and inserts it in his chest, causing himself and his house to be incinerated. Three months later, the Locke family moved to Matheson, Massachusetts, after family patriarch Rendell's murder. Youngest Locke son Bode comes across the well house, where he hears the voice of a woman claiming to be his echo.

02
TRAPPER/KEEPER

The Locke children find their mother has no recollection of being trapped in the mirror dimension. At school, Tyler becomes interested in Jackie, while Kinsey struggles to fit in until she meets Scot and others of the "Savini squad", who are trying to make a budget horror film, and cautiously joins them. Bode fears that Echo may come back and bonds with Rufus, the young Keyhouse groundskeeper.

03
HEAD GAMES

Tyler and Kinsey went Bode's mind, which resembles a fantastical arcade, and Bode shows them a memory of Rendell. Echo travels to the burned-down home where the man killed himself with a key, and she recovers the undamaged key. The next day, Tyler lets Kinsey use the Head Key to enter her mind, manifested as a distorted shopping mall, to find more memories of their father.

04
KEEPER OF THE KEYS

Kinsey has become more open and engaging at school as she tells Tyler she had used the Head Key to take out the manifestation of her fear from her mind. Talking more with Ellie, Nina learns that most of Rendell's friends are dead, with Mark Cho recently killed in a house fire; outside of Ellie, only Erin Voss is alive, but she lives in a psychiatric facility.

05
FAMILY TREE

Tyler and Kinsey are drawn to another hidden key, which they find is the Music Box Key that can only be used when inserted into a music box and allows the wielder to control a person's actions. Tyler warns Kinsey about abusing the keys' powers, not wanting to draw attention. As Tyler and Kinsey discovers about the Plant Key in the graveyard, they use it on a nearby tree and several jars are uprooted from the ground.

06
THE BLACK DOOR

Kinsey confronts Duncan about the memories, but he has no recollection of any of the events, except vaguely remembering some caves near the shore line. Kinsey convinces the Savini squad to film in those caves as a pretext to explore further. Within, she finds a door in the shape of an omega symbol, known as the Black Door.

07
DISSECTION

While waiting alone, Sam sees a picture of Keyhouse with Dodge taunting him to read Rendell's reports, which depict Sam as having mental-health issues. Feeling betrayed by the Lockes, it is implied this is what led to his murder of Rendell. Sam then takes the Lockes hostage, demanding they turn over the Head Key. Kinsey claims it is buried outside, directing him to where she buried her fear monster.

08
RAY OF F*CKING SUNSHINE

Nina begins drinking in the wake of recent events, which causes her to start to remember some of the events involving the keys. She uses the Mending Key, which allows a cabinet to restore damaged objects to their original form. Tyler and Kinsey travel to the cave with the Black Door and find a list of the "Keepers of the Keys" written on the wall that includes Rendell and his friends.

09
ECHOES

The Locke children confront Ellie about Lucas, and she explains that years prior, her boyfriend Lucas had been possessed by something that came through the Black Door and had killed two of their friends before Rendell killed him. Ellie and her friends hid all evidence of this, including removing Duncan's memories, and split up and hid the keys to prevent anyone from using them again.

10
CROWN OF SHADOWS

The battle over the Omega Key leads to decisions that can't be undone — and a dangerous door that promises plenty of trouble ahead. Now possessing both the Shadow Key and the Crown of Shadows, Dodge is able to conjure powerful shadow monsters, and she sends them to attack Keyhouse in search of the Omega Key. Tyler and Kinsey send her back through the Black Door with the help of Scot, Gabe, Jackie, and Eden.
